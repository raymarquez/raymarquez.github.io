Link to Tableau Workbook

INSIGHTers utilized Tableau in order to analyze and visualize the following data...

- Treemap to show the average loan amount per state

- Bar charts to show the amount of very low-income, low-income and moderate-income customers grouped by state

- Bar charts to show the top and bottom 10 states in terms of loan activity for the varying demographic groups represented (Native American, Asian, African American, Hawaiian/Pacific Islander, Hispanic, Caucasian)

- Symbol maps to show the top 10 states giving loans to very low-income, low-income and moderate-income customers
