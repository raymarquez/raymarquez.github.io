USDA Direct Loan vs USDA Guaranteed Loans

  • Two types of Section 502 Single-Family Rural Housing Loans
  • USDA created both loan programs to boost homeownership in rural and suburban areas
  • The two programs have key differences and are meant for two very different situations:
    • Guaranteed Loan: Meant for low- to moderate-income families
    • Direct Loan: Meant for very low-income families
  • Primary difference between USDA Direct Loans and USDA Guaranteed Loans is who funds the actual loan:
    • Guaranteed Loan: Private lenders fund the loan while the USDA backs each loan against default
    • Direct Loan: the USDA acts as the lender
